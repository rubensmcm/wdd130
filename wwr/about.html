HISTORY

Founded in 1995, Whitewater Rafting Adventures began near the Desolation Canyon River in Utah, started by outdoor enthusiast native explorers. With just two rafts, their goal was to offer thrilling yet safe rafting experiences while promoting environmental conservation.

By 2005, the company had grown significantly, attracting visitors from across the U.S. Their popular multi-day trips, featuring rafting, camping, and hiking, earned them a reputation as one of Utah's premier adventure companies! Whitewater Rafting Adventures remains committed to safety, excitement, and environmental education to this day.
